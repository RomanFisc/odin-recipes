Super Easy Salad

Description

This is the world's easiest salad that I make when I don't want to think or make anything. Makes Salad for 2

Ingredients

  • Mixed Greens
  • Half Cucumber
  • Half Pepper
  • 4 Celery Stalks
  • 3 Big Carrots
  • Dressing of choice

Steps

  1. Wash Lettuce
  2. Cut vegetables into desired size
  3. Add to lettuce in bowl, dress, and eat
