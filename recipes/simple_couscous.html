Easy Pearl Couscous Recipe

Description

This is a simple Couscous recipe I have tinkered with to make something good and easy on a weeknight to pair with lefotver chicken, lamb, etc. This dish comes together in 15 minutes or so and makes a good amount for 2 people.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Shallot
  • 1 cup Pearl Couscous
  • 1 tbsp Tumeric
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Coriander
  • 1 1/2 cup Chicken Stock
  • Salt to tast
  • 1/2 head of Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Dice shallots
  2. Heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a pot
  3. Add diced shallots and cook for about 2 mins until fragrant
  4. Add couscous with spices: tumeric, cumin, coriander, and toast until the spices are fragrant
  5. One spices are fragrant add the chicken stock. Bring to a boil
  6. Once the broth is boiling, reduce to a simmer, cover and cook for around 7 minutes or until the liquid is evaporated
  7. While the pot is simmering chop the parley
  8. When finished add the parsley into the pot, mix and serve
